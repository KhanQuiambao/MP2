Introduction

Jitensha is an online and retail bike shop based in Canterbury, Kent. Our key bike brands include Santa Cruz, Evil, Genesis, Nukeproof, Kona, Marin, Orange, Ibis, Merida, Rondo, Revel and more.

Established in 2023 we have gained a reputation for stocking high-end mountain bikes, components, accessories and cycle clothing and for our fast delivery. We are a fully independent rider-owned company. With an online and physical presence we are a leading destination for the best mountain bike products in the UK.

Company History

The Jitensha story all began with Biketape, a clear protective tape used to protect bikes from chips and scratches. Frustrated with damage to his bikes, Adam here set out to find the best quality tape possible and Biketape was born over 12 years ago.

Continuing the theme of customising bikes, new products were then launched including titanium bolts. With a growing product collection Biketart was born in the summer of 2008.

Our Location

Located near SM Bataan in the beautiful Balanga City, Bataan, our headquarters and showroom is the base of our online operations and also provides bike servicing in the large workshop here.

You can come and browse bikes, clothing, parts and accessories in our showroom. Our friendly staff are all riders and it is a relaxed environment free from push sales people.

We are across the road from one of the best mountain biking locations in the area: Covert and Covet woods. The extensive trail network there provides an excellent opportunity for customers to test ride our demo bikes or just go for a ride whilst visiting. Strava and Trailforks have the trails mapped out but ask for some guidance if you'd like to know what would suit you best or for the current weather conditions.

We have a fully equipped workshop at our showroom which provide a full range of bike maintenance from a standard bike service, repairs and complete custom builds. We are able to offer a quick turn-around on workshop jobs as we have so many parts kept in stock.
